## Instructions

### 1. **Download TunnelBear**
   - Begin by downloading TunnelBear from their official website: [TunnelBear](https://www.tunnelbear.com/).

### 2. **Log In or Create an Account**
   - Once downloaded, open the app, and you'll be prompted to log in or create a new account. Click **"Create an Account"** to proceed.

### 3. **Account Creation**
   - After clicking **"Create an Account"**, you'll be taken to a page like this:

   ![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8bbca8cd-4f99-4690-b896-c9f44679d675)

### 4. **Use a Temporary Email (Recommended)**
   - When asked for an email address, you can use a temporary email for quick setup. To do so, go to [Temp-Mail](https://temp-mail.org/en/) to get a disposable email.

### 5. **Copy the Temporary Email**
   - Once on [Temp-Mail](https://temp-mail.org/en/), you'll see a page with a temporary email address like the one shown below:

   ![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4ad6374f-0e14-4260-8264-d306ce9cf02c)
